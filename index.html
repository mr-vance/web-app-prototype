ABOUT

Puno is the all rounded fintech solution you need.

With us, the small amount of money in your pocket could make a difference in a farmer's life.

Zero Hunger

We aim to end hunger, achieve food security, and improved nutrition by promoting sustainable agriculture.

Decent work and economic growth

By empowering one farmer at a time we aim to promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth.

Responsible consumption and production

We also ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.

ABOUT US

Investors can choose the farmer and keep track of their Investment!

The Puno solution helps investors choose which farmer they want to invest in, and allows them the opportuntity to track their investment. As opposed to traditional financial institutions, farmers pay according to their harvest cycles and not monthly.
  • We are building the African small scale farming ecosystem, 1 farmer at a time. De-risking them for investment.
  • We exist for the purpose of driving these small-scale farmers into commercialization, while encouraging them to commit to climate-smart farming practices.
  • Our founder started out as a farmer; that combined with Computer Science is how Puno was born.
